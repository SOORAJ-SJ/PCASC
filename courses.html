How to Apply

Application forms can be had from the office on cash payment of Rs.100/- or by post on Demand Draft for Rs.125/- drawn in favour of the PILATHARA CO- OPERATIVE ARTS AND SCIENCE COLLEGE, PILATHARA.

Application filled in all respects shall reach the office on or before the prescribed date. No column in the application shall be left blank. Incomplete application will not be accepted.

Admission

Rank list candidates will be published on the college notice board. On receipt of intimation, the candidates shall be present for interview along with their guardian with all relevant certificates in original. The fee as required is to be remitted at the time of admission itself.
